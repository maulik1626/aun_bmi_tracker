Privacy Policy

Introduction

AUN BMI Tracker ("the App") is developed and maintained by Maulik Raja, operating as AUN Creations. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data when you use AUN BMI Tracker.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

About the App

AUN BMI Tracker is a health and fitness utility that allows you to:

  • Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) using height and weight inputs
  • Create and manage multiple profiles (e.g., family members)
  • View your BMI history through charts and trends
  • Receive general health tips based on BMI category

Data We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All BMI calculations, profile information (name, height, weight, age, gender), and history data are stored exclusively on your device using local device storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party by us.

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Location data
  • Device contacts, photos, or files
  • Health data beyond what you manually enter into the App

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads. This data collection is governed by Google's privacy policies and may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location (derived from IP)
  • Device type, operating system version, and app usage information
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

For full details on how Google collects and processes data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).

Data Retention

Your BMI records and profiles remain on your device until you delete them within the App or uninstall the App. We have no ability to access or delete this data on your behalf.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App may be used to track BMI for children by a parent or guardian who manages the profile.
